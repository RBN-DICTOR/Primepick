Our Story

Our Story & Vision

Founded with a vision to redefine luxury real estate in Kenya, Prime Picks Properties represents the pinnacle of property excellence. Our journey began over 15 years ago with a simple mission: to connect discerning clients with premium properties that exceed expectations.

Today, we stand as Kenya's leading luxury real estate advisory, combining deep market expertise with a commitment to delivering exceptional property solutions. Our team of experts brings together extensive knowledge of real estate markets, ensuring our clients receive personalized service and valuable insights.

We specialize in connecting discerning clients with premium properties that match their lifestyle aspirations and investment goals across prime locations in Kenya's most sought-after neighborhoods.
