Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you serve? +

We specialize in premium properties across Nairobi's most sought-after neighborhoods including Westlands, Kilimani, Kileleshwa, Riverside, Karen, and Lavington. We also handle select properties in Mombasa's coastal region and upcoming developments in major Kenyan cities.

How quickly can I view a property? +

We can typically arrange viewings within 24-48 hours of your inquiry. For urgent viewings, we offer same-day appointments subject to property availability and occupant consent. Virtual tours are available immediately for most properties.

Do you assist with financing? +

Yes, we partner with leading financial institutions and mortgage providers to help secure favorable financing options for our clients. Our financial advisory team can guide you through the mortgage application process and connect you with suitable lenders based on your profile.

What fees do you charge? +

Our fees vary based on the service provided. For property purchases, we typically charge a commission percentage based on the sale price. For rentals and executive stays, our fees are usually included in the quoted price. We provide transparent fee breakdowns before any commitments are made.

Can you help with property management? +

Absolutely. We offer comprehensive property management services including tenant screening, rent collection, maintenance coordination, and regular property inspections. Our management services ensure your investment is well-maintained and generates optimal returns.
